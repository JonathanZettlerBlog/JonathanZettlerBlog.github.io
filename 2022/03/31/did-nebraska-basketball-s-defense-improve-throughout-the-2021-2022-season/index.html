Did Nebraska Basketball's Defense Improve Throughout the 2021-2022 Season

2022-03-31

It’s no secret that Nebraska had a rough basketball season this year going 10-22 overall and 4-16 in the Big Ten. The team continued to drop games because of poor defense, but did the Cornhuskers defense improve at all throughout the season?

Defense clearly was a struggle for the team as many fans pointed out that opposing teams would have their best shooting nights against the Cornhuskers. Did Nebraska’s opponents really shoot that well against them?

To get a good look at how Nebraska’s defense changed throughout the season, I made line chart of how Nebraska’s opponents true shooting was against them over the course of the season.

Forcing turnovers is a huge part of good defensive basketball, specifically getting steals. This scatterplot below gives you a good look at how many steals Nebraska’s defense got against each team over the course of the season to see whether or not they improved or got worse with steals over the season.

As you can see, the Huskers did not show any improvement in forcing their opponents to turn the ball over. While they had more steals in their last game than their first game, they did not show any consistent improvement in steals throughout the season.
